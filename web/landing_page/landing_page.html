Describe. Deposit. Discover.

Desposit

  • Seamless deposition to repositories
  • Makes metadata labelling simple
  • Compatibility with commonly used repositories

Citations and Reproducibility

  • Receive credit for more than just papers
  • Profiles aggregate research objects such as data, source code, images, posters which can then be referenced
  • Links to analysis platforms (iPlant / Galaxy) allow workflows to be rerun

Linked Data

  • Semantic meaning attached to objects
  • Discoverabilty of research outputs by traversal of knowledge graphs
  • Data mining techniques provide insight into the state of a discipline
